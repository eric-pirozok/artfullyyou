ZOOM ART CLASSES

Zoom Art classes are one hour a week set at a specific day and time. Miss Amy will email the Zoom login information and list of supplies needed the weekend before the class.
If interested, please contact Miss Amy at (amy@beartfullyyou.com or text only to 509-542-7779)

SMALL ZOOM ART CLASSES
Small Zoom classes consist of 2 to 4 students of similar age and/or skill level.
Students will build their art skills on the foundation elements of art and principles of design.

PRIVATE ZOOM ART CLASSES
Private Zoom classes are one-on-one with Miss Amy.
Private Zoom class is for students who may need a little more assistance, or those who desire to work on specific skills.
